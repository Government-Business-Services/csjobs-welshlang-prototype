About the job

Summary

As one of 2 HRBP's you will work to the Deputy Head of HR, working closely with Employee Services, Recruitment and Inclusion, Learning and Development and Payroll Teams that make up the HR Service to provide a robust and seamless HR Service.

To contribute to the promotion, development and delivery of a high quality HR Business Partner support service to help deliver service plans and the wider Commission Strategies with a focus on promoting best HR and OD practice.

Job description

You will be required to provide a HR Business Partner service to a number of designated customers which meets or exceeds defined service levels. This will involve providing proactive advice on workforce opportunities that supports the delivery of the business as the designated business partner.

This role will have a specific portfolio of work to lead on Policy Development and Delivery and this post will champion and lead on the development and implementation of inclusive people policies and procedures to ensure they support the workforce planning, the development of an agile workforce and promote our organisational values.

Responsibilities

- To contribute to the development/delivery of a high quality HR business partner support function throughout the Commission with a focus on promoting best HR and OD practice.

- To work closely with the Directors and their Heads of Service in assessing and anticipating workforce needs and help in delivering/commissioning integrated solutions

- Through key stakeholder relationships, devise and implement relevant HR strategies, policies and procedures which meet business requirements in addition to providing strategic HR advice.

- Acting as the trusted advisor to your Director and leadership team, influencing the business on the people agenda implementation whilst providing appropriate advice, coaching and facilitation to deliver results.

- Interpreting management information and providing business insight to drive innovation, change and improvement into the business and people practice and policy.

- To contribute to the development and delivery of HR related organisational development programmes in line with the Commission's People Strategy, including that relating to workforce and succession planning, performance management, well-being and employee engagement to ensure we maintain a high performing and inclusive workforce.

- To lead on the development and implementation of robust HR policies, procedures and business development projects to ensure they reflect both up-to-date employment law and best practice, fitting in with our vision of being an exemplar employer.

- To contribute to the development and implementation of regular HR activities and other engagement activities to provide employees and managers with the knowledge and skills to grow organisational capability.

- To promote and maintain effective employee relations including taking a support role in consultations/negotiations with trade unions and the workforce on any restructures and change programmes.

- To undertake project work and related research activity to support the development of best practice HR policy, procedures and practice, in compliance with statutory requirements and the delivery of the Commissions People Strategy and Workforce Plans.

- Such other duties and responsibilities commensurate with the grade as may reasonably be required from time to time.
